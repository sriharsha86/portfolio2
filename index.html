About Me

Welcome to My Creative World

I'm a writer, digital artist, and aspiring mangaka with a passion for storytelling, world-building, and dynamic visuals. My work blends intricate narratives with striking artwork, bringing characters and worlds to life. Currently, I'm developing my skills learning new things and new forms of drawings and arts.

Beyond storytelling, I specialize in digital art and video editing, capturing the essence of my creations through detailed illustrations and engaging visuals. I share my creative process—from concept sketches to final artwork—alongside video edits that enhance the experience.

This portfolio showcases my journey as an artist, featuring drawings, speedpaints, and story-driven animations. Whether you're here for inspiration, collaboration, or just to explore, I hope you enjoy the worlds I create.

Follow my journey as I bring my stories to life—one frame at a time.
